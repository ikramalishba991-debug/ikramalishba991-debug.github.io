Featured Projects

Bioinformatics

AI-Accelerated Pathogenicity Pipeline

The Problem: Traditional genetic analysis faces massive scalability bottlenecks. Storing and searching full gene sequences is slow and computationally expensive, hindering rapid clinical diagnosis from DNA snippets.

The Solution: A hybrid system using K-mer Indexing for sub-second gene identification and a Conv1D Neural Network to instantly classify pathogenicity.

The Impact: Bypasses the need for full-genome storage, providing a fast, intelligent, and scalable diagnostic tool that predicts clinical significance (Pathogenic/Benign) with high accuracy.

AgriTech

Smart Crop Management System (SCMS)

The Problem: Modern farmers struggle with complex multi-crop decisions. Most tools offer only single-crop analysis, failing to account for crop rotation, intercropping synergy, and volatile weather risks.

The Solution: An AI-enhanced platform integrating Real-time Weather APIs and Soil Databases to run side-by-side comparative suitability analyses.

The Impact: Empowers farmers with predictive yield models and risk mitigation strategies, leading to optimized resource use and significantly higher, more predictable agricultural yields.
